Quantum state

This circuit sets its qubit into a state that depends on the knob.

[here we will have the quantum washer]

A quantum computer won't tell us the output state directly, we only measure one of the basis states that the qubit's state collapsed to. We can repeat the procedure multiple times to get a sense of what's the probability of observing each basis state.
